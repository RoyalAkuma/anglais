MOOC : Did you say "Images" ?

Hello and Welcome on our MOOC about Images !!
Everyday we see a ton of different images and we'll try to explain what they are
and how to apply modification to them. A MOOC is a Massive Open Online Course made to teach people about a subject on the web. With media such as video, text or sound !
