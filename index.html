MOOC : Did you say "Images" ?

Hello and Welcome on our MOOC about Images !!
A MOOC is a Massive Open Online Course made to teach people about a subject on the web with multimedia contents! Everyday we see a ton of different images for instance advertisement, photographs and as long as you can see, everything you see are images ! On computer we are able to change and apply modification to them throught different technique and rendering. This course is about Images !

We have prepared four courses about Images and you are free to jump from one to another thanks to the bar that is on top of every part ! Each part is made of a multimedia content, a written text about the part and finaly a short quizz about the course you just read !
